#HairStory
Index Story Submit FAQ
Why would people talk about hair?
Because hair is one of the most visible and personal parts of our appearance, but it says a lot about a person.
How open is this project?
Hairstory isn’t limited to this website or to one format. You can create your own version in any medium such as images, video, audio, writing, print, or digital. You can design it however you want, with any layout or structure that makes sense to you.
Can I submit my HairStory by myself?
Yes, you can send your HairStory by hitting Submit.
Can I just simply host my stories locally?
You can host it anywhere you want. HairStory isn’t a fixed archive; it’s an open prompt. The project grows through however you choose to interpret and share it.
Can I use the stories here as an inspiration?
The stories here are meant to spark ideas, not set rules. You can borrow the structure, respond to a theme, or reinterpret an approach in your own way.
How to recognize a HairStory?
You can include the HairStory logo on their post or piece.
